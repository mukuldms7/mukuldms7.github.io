QUALITY SYSTEEM

RAW MATERIAL AND INCOMING PARTS CONTROL

Raw material is procured from standard and established sources. The incoming material is subjected to appropriate metallurgical, chemical and mechanical testing as per sample plans.

Raw material is stored in designated areas.

Alphanumeric schemes for identifying various grades of raw material is established.

All incoming semi finished and finished items are subjected to receipt inspection for dimensional accuracy, metallurgical performance and functional testing.

Inspection and acceptance norms are fixed and a system for disposing off the rejected material is established.

WPS , PQR & WPQ:

During the manufacturing operations the inspection status of each stage are marked.
