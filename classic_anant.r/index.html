About Us


Taking inspiration from DefExpo-88 for indigenization, Chetan Dharia established ANANT INDUSTRIES in 1988.

Now, with rich experience of 30 years, Anant Industries has become a reputed source for Various Indian Defence Organizations
for Hydraulic and Pneumatic Assemblies for T72 / T90 / BMP Tanks and its Engines.

With our dedication for Quality, Cost and Delivery towards our Customers,
we are ever expanding our possibilities and opportunities for an even more prosperous future.
