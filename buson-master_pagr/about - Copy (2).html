Who are We?

We are a team of clinicians striving to simplify workflow communication in hospitals through Pagr, a team collaboration platform tailored for healthcare.

We, as doctors, have seen our surroundings evolve through many spheres of development. But an aspect that has not advanced proportionally is the communication infrastructure between healthcare professionals. Through Pagr, we aspire to change that.

Our Story

Where we began

During our clinical practice, we realised that our healthcare communication is disorganised and fragmented across multiple channels and devices like landlines, WhatsApp, emails, paper files and so on. Moreover, this lack of structure results in increased time and energy spent in relaying information. How often have you seen yourself make multiple calls to follow up on a single update?

Fragmented communication has been an important cause for burnout among healthcare professionals.

When we mentioned this to our friends from other industries, they were shocked to find that the healthcare sector doesn’t have its own customised communication channel the way they all do. This is when we decided to stop waiting for someone else to come around and rescue us and instead, started building one ourselves.

We wanted to establish a platform that would bring efficiency to healthcare communication and improve the quality of work life for healthcare professionals.

The next few months were spent in RnD and surveying what our clinicians needed. We got some great inputs, and a few unsual ones.

“I am exhausted from juggling between patient files, whatsapp, landlines and mobile calls”, a resident confessed.

“I’m tired of making multiple calls to find out which of my residents is on-duty, it is quite time consuming and frankly frustrating”, a physician complained.

“I wish there was an easier way to reach residents than calling from landlines!”, a nurse chipped in.

“Holograms!!! So even we can work from home!” a junior resident joked.

The Journey So Far

We took these inputs and set out on a journey to pave a new way for our healthcare system. Step by step and feature by feature, with a team of doctors and coders, we worked from ground up to address each issue.

So, after months of intense research and development, infinite coffee runs, and late night brainstorming sessions, Pagr was born. We aim to support our medical fraternity at all times. We hope to make Pagr a product that is constantly evolving just like its surroundings. And with your help, we intend to make it better everyday.
