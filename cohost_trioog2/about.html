Your “3rd Eye”

Keeping this in mind we created TRIOOG.

TRIOOG, is a Personal Alert Mode Security System which consists of a mobile application, a hardware component and a strong backend network comprised of automatic data compression and quick transfer with a strong protection of cyber security.

The idea is that you can immediately notify your loved ones that you are in danger with sharing of your live location as well as pictures, and audio for additional assistance. Along with many more unique and advanced features designed to provide support in all possible dire situations.
