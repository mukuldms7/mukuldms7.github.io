Unlike other messaging apps, Pagr is made specifically for healthcare professionals; which is why Pagr caters to your needs better than anyone else. Each feature is developed to address an existing problem that clinicians face on a day-to-day basis and is tested to ensure the problem is solved. The features and app are constantly enhanced with time, through the feedback we receive from our doctors because our one goal remains the same; to make doctors’ lives easier.



Whether it’s urgent phone calls, communication between residents and nurses, or taking a breather from the beeps of Whatsapp and Messenger, Pagr solves it all. It is specially designed to cater to the medical community, to make workflow simple, clean, and efficient. It also uncomplicates the issue of work-life balance in which messages get misplaced and important notifications from your team often are left at the bottom of your chat boxes! Therefore, patient care is top priority for Pagr; no matter what the situation might be.
