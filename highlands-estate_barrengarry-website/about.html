PRESTIGIOUS ACREAGE LOTS FOR LUXURY LIVING

200 acres of lush green country with stunning escarpment views, dams, rn=unning creeks and an abundance of wildlife all to be enjoyed in this lifestyle developement.

Surrounded by glorious escarpments the region is rich in flora and fauna, with sparkling rivers and creeks. The region abounds in fertile farmland with meadows that expand to undulating grasslands and pastures beyond the river flats.

ONLY 2 HOURS AWAY

Nestled between the Barrengarry and Cambewarra mountains, just a stone's throw north of the historically rich town of Kangaroo valley. It is a pleasant drive from the southern highlands and nowra, approximately two hours drive from both Sydney and Canberra.

This magnificent region is close enough for a weekend getaway while feeling a million miles away. Spread throughout the area prestigious homes are set on sprawling estates and meandering lane ways lead to quaint country cottages.
