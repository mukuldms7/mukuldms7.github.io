About Pratik Copper

Pratik Copper is led by high class modern technologies, able team and manufacturing capacity and has become brand to depend on. From designs authenticity, product durability, manufacturing precision and long term customer associations we complete the growing value chain of copper utensils and copper market.

With a legacy of over half a century we have led the change in copper utensil markets. Pratik has pioneered in manufacturing over 200 types of products and also have presence in industrials copper and elite copper utensils.

From manufacturing to customer reach we currently are capable of handling 1.5 metric ton per day and in next two years will grow 3 times from our current capacity.

While preserving the ancient tradition of making copper utensils, we have now spread our wings deploying high end technology and contemporary designs to open for you a new world of copper.

Join hands with us and explore the possibilities of serving with community with good health through our tradition of “Pratik Copper”
