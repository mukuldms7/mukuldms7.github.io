About Acharya Atre

Acharya Atre was born on August 13, 1898 in Saswad, Pune, India.

Prahlad Keshav Atre is considered a shocking personality in Maharashtra. He made his mark in various fields like Literature, Education, Drama, Film, Politics and Journalism. In all these areas, he had a tremendous influence. He had a tremendous legacy on public opinion. They were farewell speakers. With their oratory, they used to thrust the audience.
