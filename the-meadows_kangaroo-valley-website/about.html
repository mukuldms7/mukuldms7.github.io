About The Meadows

THE MEADOWS

Own a piece of your dream! Acreage lots for lifestyle living.

98 acres of lush green country with horse riding trails, running creeks and an abundance of wildlife all to be enjoyed in this lifestyle development.

SOMETHING MAGICAL

The Meadows is located in Kangaroo Valley.

The region is famous for its waterfalls, outdoor adventures, delicious food and wines. Kangaroo Valley evokes a sense of country charm with its colonial history, a heritage listed Pub and has Australia’s last surviving wooden suspension bridge.

ONLY 2 HOURS AWAY

Kangaroo Valley is located between the Southern Highlands and the NSW South Coast.

ABOUT TWO HOURS’ DRIVE FROM BOTH SYDNEY AND CANBERRA.

Accommodation in Kangaroo Valley ranges from luxury spa retreats to holiday cottages and campgrounds.

LIFESTYLE SUBDIVISION

  • 98 ACRES with surrounding mountain ranges
  • Development of 1 and 2 acre lifestyle lots
  • Access to Private bushland for bushwalking, mountain biking and horse riding
  • Native scenery with opportunities to spot wildlife
