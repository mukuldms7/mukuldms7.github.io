The Problem

Explanation

Lack of a convenient and structured communication platform for healthcare professionals to discuss sensitive data. Communicating patient information on Phone, SMS, Whatsapp is not only unsafe but also extremely inconvenient and often causes errors in telephonic orders. Moreover the process is extremely slow owing to passage of information through multiple stations.

Example

For example,

  • A resident doctor in a hospital calls the laboratory to ask for the reports of an admitted patient.
  • He then calls the Physician to inform the reports.
  • The Physician then gives a telephonic order to the resident who then conveys it to the nursing staff who then calls the pharmacy to order the advised medication.

Summary

Here the information has passed from laboratory to the resident doctor to the Physician and then from Physician to the Resident doctor to the nursing to the Pharmacy. With increase in number of stations, the speed of flow of information decreases and possibility of error increases.
