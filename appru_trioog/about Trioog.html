About Us

It is extremely disturbing to see how abductions, rape and murder are increasing at an alarming rate in India. Personal safety is what every parent, spouse and family want; and lives get shattered when this is taken away from them.

Keeping this in mind we created TRIOOG. The word ‘OOG’ in Dutch means ‘Eye’ and ‘Tri’ in Greek means ‘3’. TRIOOG, is a Personal Alert Mode Security system that uses a tap-activated wearable device which triggers an alarm when the wearer taps the SOS button when in distress. This alarm is sent through a mobile app to the wearer’s emergency contact list; who then can form a virtual control room that can swing into action to protect the wearer at their time in need.

TRIOOG notifies your family and your emergency contacts with an SOS signal with the wearer’s Real-time location along with Audio/Video/Photo uploads when the wearer activates their SOS signal. Thus, your near and dear one’s will not only have the wearer’s real-time location access; but also, at the same time they will have audio, video and photo evidence of your assailant. All data is secured and encrypted for your safety and privacy.

TRIOOG as the name suggests will act as your “3rd Eye”.

We at TRIOOG want to reach out and empower our community to “Never Feel Helpless”
