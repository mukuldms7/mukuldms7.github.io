Why a Smartwatch for Security?

Why the hardware component not in the form of a mobile, pendant, bracelet, etc.

1. Easily accessible device and SOS button.

In times of crisis, when a SOS needs to be sent urgently, the watch is easily on hand. Accessing the device and the SOS button on it is very simple and least time consuming compared with the other items like a mobile or a pendant.

2. Discreet pressing of SOS button possible.

Pressing of the SOS button on the watch can well go unnoticed; whereas removing the mobile or the other items and looking for the SOS button can attract unwanted attention of the persecutor.

3. Discreet recording of audio, video and photos.

To record the audio or video and to take photos of the surroundings we have to flash the mobile and hold it in a certain way. This is not the case with the watch.

4. Remains on body even during struggle.

As the watch is securely in place because of the belt, it is not likely to fall away during a tussle or to be taken away by the persecutor.

5. Removal of watch can be detected.

Due to the program designed in the app, a forcible removal of the watch is detectable and in itself acts as a warning, not so in the case of the other items.
