About

Pragati Engineering

Pragati Engineering is one of the leading Press Tool Design & Manufacturing Company, established in the year 2004. We manufacture quality dies, fixtures as per Customer’s Requirements.

At Pragati, our dedication to customers is rooted in a deep sense of responsibility that is shared by all of our employees.

Pragati Engineering, an ISO 9001: 2008 certified division caters to the entire tooling solutions and has a National reach.

We have well Qualified, Dynamic Engineers in design department and tool room division. We also have skilled manpower. We follow straight quality control measures at every stage of manufacturing process. We have competency in manufacturing of Press tools fixtures and panel checkers for various critical sheet metal components and assemblies.

With new technologies, facilities and experience in press tooling, we are confident to cater any requirements in sheet metal.
