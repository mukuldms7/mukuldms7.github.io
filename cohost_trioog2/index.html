Why in the form of a watch?

And why the hardware component not in the form of a mobile, pendant, bracelet, etc.

1. Easily accessible device and SOS button.

In times of crisis, when a SOS needs to be sent urgently, the watch is easily on hand. Accessing the device and the SOS button on it is very simple and least time consuming compared with the other items like a mobile or a pendant.

2. Discreet pressing of SOS button possible.

Pressing of the SOS button on the watch can well go unnoticed; whereas removing the mobile or the other items and looking for the SOS button can attract unwanted attention of the persecutor.

3. Discreet recording of audio, video and photos.

To record the audio or video and to take photos of the surroundings we have to flash the mobile and hold it in a certain way. This is not the case with the watch.

4. Remains on body even during struggle.

As the watch is securely in place because of the belt, it is not likely to fall away during a tussle or to be taken away by the persecutor.

5. Removal of watch can be detected.

Due to the program designed in the app, a forcible removal of the watch is detectable and in itself acts as a warning, not so in the case of the other items.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the launch of TRIOOG ?

We are planning the beta launch of the app and the system in January 2020 and complete launch in March 2020 along with the TRIOOG Chakra smartwatch. Sign-up above to get early access and updates regarding the launch.

What will be the range of the app? Will it work throughout the country?

Range of the app with the phone will depend on the range of the Bluetooth connection. The watch will work anywhere in the world wherever (or as long as) there is cellular network.

Why is the watch named TRIOOG Chakra?

Chakra in Sanskrit means 'Circle'. The main aim of TRIOOG is to keep the wearer within 'Its Circle of Protection'. TRIOOG is a combination of the Greek word 'Tri' which is the number '3' and 'oog' which in Dutch means eye. According to yoga traditions, The 3rd Eye Chakra is one of seven Kundalini Chakras. This Chakra is related to the 'Supreme element', which is the combination of all the elements in their pure form. Simply speaking, TRIOOG Chakra aims to be your 3rd-Eye Chakra.

Will the watch be compatible with android/iOS mobile phones?

We will launch with android compatibility. Software update for iOS compatibility will be released in a couple of months.

What will be the cost of TRIOOG?

We want this TRIOOG to reach and benefit maximum number of people. That is why, the targeted price of smartwatch will be between Rs. 15,000 - Rs. 20,000 ($215-$285). The app will work on a freemium model. Basic functions will be available for free. Advanced premium functions will be available on subscription basis. The subscription will be between Rs. 100 - Rs. 150 ($1.5-$2.5) per month.

Will it work if the mobile phone is out of coverage area?

If network to the watch is provided through the phone then it will work as long as the mobile is in coverage area. If sim card is inserted into the watch then that sim card will have to be in coverage area. In either cases, to minimize the risk of lack of functioning due to loss of network, we are designing a robust system with AI, which will predict the upcoming loss of network in the coming minutes and will automatically switch to alert mode and SOS signal will be sent if the system doesn’t receive any response from the watch in a pre-decided amount of time.
