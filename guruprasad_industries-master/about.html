About Us

Passionate Craftsmanship in Every Scent We Create

Incepted in the year 2010, Guruprasad Perfumery Works is an eminent entity indulged in manufacturing a huge compilation of concentrate fragrances for Agarbatti, Dhoop, Cosmetics, Detergent & Household products. We manufacture our products with the use of supreme in class material & progressive tools & technology, this are conformism with the norms & guidelines defined by the market. Along with this these are tested a set of norms prior final delivery of the order
