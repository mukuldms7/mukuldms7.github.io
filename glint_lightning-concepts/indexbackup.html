Our Approach

We focus on the design and creation of innovative and contemporary lighting solution for spaces. Our business operates with amalgamation of lighting design, engineering and ecological practices and principles. We are committed to the preservation and sustain ability of natural resources and all our efforts are carefully coordinated to ensure minimum disruption and harm to the environment.

Each and every project is designed comprehensively by using sustainable sources; we transform and create memorable, functional and uniquely designed spaces for a portfolio of highly discerning clients.

Pathfinder

Lighting Concepts Pvt. Ltd is focused on end to end advisory services in Lighting Design & Consultancy.

With a formal education in Electronics Engineering from Mumbai and Lighting Design Certification from US and Germany, Ms.Ekta Sanghvvi has assisted various International Lighting Designers in Dubai before giving birth to LIGHTING CONCEPTS & Lighting Concepts Private Limited in 2004 and 2013 respectively in Pune, after achieving a quality experience in solar industry.

She is internationally known for both her theoretical and practical knowledge about architectural lighting. Each of her dynamic and innovative assignments builds on over 10 years of innovative exploration and research in the interconnected fields of architecture and design.

Ekta's interest lies in the careful edge between internal and external lighting along with all aspects of natural lighting. Her practice integrates natural lighting and innovative lighting systems, leading to cutting-edge energy efficient technologies directly allowing conservation of resources.

According to her lighting is a combination of both Art and Science and good lighting plan is a plan having layers of light, satisfying the user's multifunctional needs complimenting the design essence of the space.

She has not only participated in various exhibitions, conferences and workshops in India but also across the world and is an active member of IESNA (Illuminating Engineering Society of North America). She has actively guided the Lighting Summit 2014 in Acetech Mumbai as a knowledge partner.

She is also a visiting faculty in different schools of architecture.She has designed multitude of projects ranging from individual residences to large townships, work spaces, Special Economic Zones (SEZ) and IT parks, Residential & Commercial complexes, Restaurants & Hotels. She also assists Government Projects in Master Planning with Lighting across a few cities.

Presently she is associated with several leading international architects and interior designers for various upcoming projects across the world.

She continues to strive in her pursuits towards excellence in this field.
