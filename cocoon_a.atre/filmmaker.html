The Filmmaker

Atre's film career is also huge.

He wrote the scripts of 'Bramhachari', "Brandychi Batli" which were directed by Master Vinayak. His other great films include, 'Dharmaveer', 'Payachi Dasi'. He established the institute 'Atre Pictures'. Based on the story of Sane Guruji, he produced and directed the film 'Shyamchi Aai'. This film received the President's gold medal. Besides these, he also produced the film on the life of Mahatma Phule. Most of the Atre's films contain a flamboyant mix of humor and social content. He also produced Hindi films. Tribute written after Jawaharlal Nehru's death by Atre are considered Unparalleled.
