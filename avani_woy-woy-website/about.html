About Avani

BOUTIQUE RESIDENCES FOR LIFESTYLE LIVING

The Avondale Property Group is excited to present Avani. Avani is set to provide a benchmark for lifestyle residences on the Central Coast.

Avani is a boutique development of high quality residences normally reserved for the elite, but now a place you can call home.

HOME IS WHERE THE HEART IS

Avani is perfectly located to take advantage of an array of outdoor activities, sample local delicacies nearby cafes and restaurants, or explore nature at the national parks. It offers residencesthe perfect lifestyle living with all modern amenities within an arm’s reach.

ONLY 60 MINS AWAY

Avani is located in Woy Woy which is an easy 1 hour drive from Sydney city and less then90 minutes to Newcastle. Woy Woy has been a popular tourist destination for over 130 years.

It is a coastal town and sits on the Brisbane waters, close to Ettalong, Umina, and Avoca beaches.

BOUTIQUE RESIDENCES

DA approved project of 34 Boutique Residences

  • 27 Apartments (1, 2 & 3 bedrooms)
  • 7 Townhouses (3 bedrooms) each with individual lock up garages
  • Fully secured basement parking for apartments and visitors
  • Communal Roof top terrace and BBQ area
  • Short 250m walk to the waterfront
  • 750m from major shopping complex and Woy Woy CBD
