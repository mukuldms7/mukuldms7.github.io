Frequently Asked Questions

What will be the range of the app? Will it work throughout the country?

Range of the app with the phone will depend on the range of the Bluetooth connection. The app will work anywhere in the world wherever (or as long as) there is cellular network.

Will it work if the mobile phone is out of coverage area?

If network to the watch is provided through the phone then it will work as long as the mobile is in coverage area. If sim card is inserted into the watch then that sim card will have to be in coverage area. In either cases, to minimize the risk of lack of functioning due to loss of network, we are designing a robust system with AI, which will predict the upcoming loss of network in the coming minutes and will automatically switch to alert mode and SOS signal will be sent if the system doesnâ€™t receive any response from the watch in a pre-decided amount of time.
